I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. As a member of the PL/FM/SE Group, I am being advised by Prof. Lingming Zhang. I'm generously supported by a Two Sigma PhD Fellowship.
Previously, I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science from Turing Class, Peking University.
